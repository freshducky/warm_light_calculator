## Description (4,000 characters max)

**Warm Light Calculator - Your Eye-Friendly Companion for Everyday Math**

Tired of harsh, bright calculator screens that strain your eyes? Warm Light Calculator is designed with your comfort in mind, featuring a soothing warm light theme that's easy on the eyes during long calculations.

**Perfect for Restaurants & Services**
Skip the mental math at restaurants! Our built-in tip calculator makes splitting bills effortless. Choose from 15%, 20%, or 25% tip options with one tap, and see your tip breakdown displayed clearly. Perfect for servers, diners, and anyone who needs quick tip calculations.

**Key Features:**
• Tip Calculator - Quick 15%, 20%, and 25% tip buttons with automatic breakdown
• Warm Light Theme - Soothing color palette designed to reduce eye strain
• Multiple Themes - Choose from Warm Light, High Contrast, and Dark Mode
• Accessibility First - Text-to-speech, high contrast modes, large touch targets, and sound effects
• Memory Functions - Store, recall, and manage calculations with MC, MR, M+, M- buttons
• Undo Function - Made a mistake? Easily undo your last action
• Precise Currency Calculations - All monetary values rounded to cents for accuracy
• Clean, Modern Interface - Intuitive design that's both beautiful and functional

**Why Warm Light Calculator?**

Whether you're calculating tips at a restaurant, managing expenses, or solving everyday math problems, Warm Light Calculator provides a comfortable, accessible experience. The warm light theme isn't just a design choice—it's a feature that makes extended use more pleasant, especially in low-light conditions.

**Real-World Use Cases:**

For Restaurant Staff: Quickly calculate tips for multiple tables. The one-tap tip buttons save precious seconds during busy shifts. See the exact tip amount and total before presenting the bill.

For Diners: Split the bill with friends, calculate tips accurately, and never second-guess your math. The tip breakdown shows exactly how much you're tipping.

For Students: Study late into the night without eye strain. The warm light theme reduces blue light exposure that can interfere with sleep. Perfect for late-night homework sessions.

For Professionals: Manage expenses, calculate project costs, and track budgets with confidence. Memory functions let you store intermediate calculations for complex financial planning.

**Accessibility & Inclusivity**

We believe everyone deserves access to great tools. Warm Light Calculator includes comprehensive accessibility features:
- Text-to-speech for voice feedback
- High contrast modes for better visibility
- Large touch targets for easier interaction
- Sound effects for tactile feedback
- Full VoiceOver compatibility

**Privacy Promise**

We take your privacy seriously. Warm Light Calculator operates entirely offline. Your calculations never leave your device. No tracking, no data collection, no advertising. Just a calculator that respects your privacy.

**The Warm Light Advantage**

The warm light theme isn't just aesthetic—it's functional. Research shows that warm color temperatures reduce eye strain and can help maintain natural sleep rhythms. While other calculators bombard you with bright whites and blues, Warm Light Calculator provides a comfortable alternative that's easier on your eyes, especially during extended use or in dimly lit environments.

**Technical Excellence**

Built with Flutter for smooth performance across all devices. The app is optimized for speed and responsiveness, ensuring calculations happen instantly without lag. The interface adapts beautifully to different screen sizes, from iPhone SE to iPhone Pro Max.

**Perfect for All Ages**

Whether you're a student learning math, a professional managing finances, or a senior citizen who prefers comfortable interfaces, Warm Light Calculator is designed to be accessible and intuitive for everyone.

**Why Choose Warm Light Calculator?**

In a world filled with apps that prioritize flashy features over user comfort, Warm Light Calculator stands apart. We believe technology should enhance your life, not strain your eyes. We believe tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. We believe good design is invisible—it just works, beautifully.

**Get Started Now**

Download Warm Light Calculator and discover a new way to calculate. Free, no ads, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. Just a beautiful, functional calculator that cares about your comfort and privacy.

Transform your mobile math experience today. Your eyes will thank you.
