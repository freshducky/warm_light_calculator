## Sample Nomination Description

**Title:**
```
Warm Light Calculator - Eye-Friendly Calculator Launch
```

**Description:**
```
Warm Light Calculator reimagines the calculator experience with a focus on eye comfort and accessibility. Featuring a unique warm light theme designed to reduce eye strain, this app is perfect for extended use, especially during late-night calculations or in low-light environments.

Key Highlights:
• Eye-friendly warm light theme reduces blue light exposure
• Built-in tip calculator (15%, 20%, 25%) for restaurants and services
• Comprehensive accessibility features: VoiceOver support, high contrast modes, large touch targets
• Privacy-first: No ads, no tracking, entirely offline
• Multiple themes: Warm Light, High Contrast, and Dark Mode
• Professional-grade calculator with memory functions and undo capability

Why It's Special:
Unlike standard calculators that prioritize function over comfort, Warm Light Calculator was built from the ground up with user experience and accessibility in mind. The warm color palette isn't just aesthetic—research shows warm color temperatures reduce eye strain and can help maintain natural sleep rhythms.

Perfect for:
- Restaurant staff and diners calculating tips
- Students working late at night
- Professionals managing expenses
- Users with visual impairments or sensitivity to bright screens
- Anyone who values both function and comfort

This app represents a thoughtful approach to digital tool design, prioritizing user well-being and accessibility without compromising functionality.
```

**Character Count:** ~1,200 characters (adjust as needed)

## What Apple Looks For

**Editorial Features Typically Include:**

1. **Unique Story or Angle**
   - Your angle: Eye-friendly design + accessibility focus
   - Unique in the calculator space

2. **Quality Design**
   - Your app: Beautiful, thoughtful interface
   - Professional polish

3. **Innovation**
   - Your angle: First calculator focused on visual comfort
   - Accessibility-first approach

4. **Social Impact**
   - Your angle: Making technology accessible
   - Privacy-focused approach

5. **Timeliness**
   - Launch timing
   - Relevant to current trends (accessibility, privacy, eye health)

---

## Tips for Success

1. **Be Specific:** Don't just say "it's a calculator" - explain what makes it special
2. **Highlight Accessibility:** Apple values accessibility features
3. **Emphasize Design:** Your warm light theme is unique
4. **Privacy Angle:** No ads, no tracking is a differentiator
5. **User Benefit:** Focus on how it helps users (eye strain, accessibility, privacy)

---

## Timeline Expectations

- **Editorial reviews are selective:** Only a small percentage of apps get featured
- **No guarantee:** Even great apps don't always get featured
- **Timing matters:** Apple features apps that align with current themes/events
- **Keep trying:** You can submit nominations for future updates/features

---

## What Happens Next

1. **Submit your nomination** with the information above
2. **Apple's editorial team reviews** (no timeline guaranteed)
3. **If selected:** You'll be contacted about featuring
4. **If not selected:** No notification (normal process)
5. **Continue building:** Keep improving your app regardless

---

## Additional Submission Tips

**Best Practices:**
- Submit 2-4 weeks before launch (if possible)
- Update nomination if you add significant features
- Be honest and authentic in your description
- Highlight what makes your app unique
- Focus on user benefits, not just features

**Don't:**
- Oversell or exaggerate
- Compare directly to competitors negatively
- Use marketing jargon excessively
- Submit multiple nominations for the same thing
