# Warm Light Calculator

A gentle, eye-friendly calculator app designed to reduce eye strain and promote mindful digital usage.

This calculator follows a warm, low-stimulation design philosophy:

- **No blue light or vivid colors** - Uses warm browns, tans, and creams
- **Gentle on eyes** - Reduces strain and stress during use
- **Supports better sleep** - Warm colors help maintain natural circadian rhythms
- **Encourages mindful use** - Calm interface promotes focused, low-stimulation interaction
- **Accessible design** - Large text and high contrast for children and older adults
- **Digital balance** - Helps break screen addiction with soothing visual design
